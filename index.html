How to Play Blackjack

Objective

  • The goal is to have a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer without exceeding 21.
  • If your hand goes over 21, you lose immediately.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2–10) are worth their face value.
  • Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10 each.
  • An Ace can be worth either 1 or 11, depending on which value keeps your total ≤ 21.

Gameplay

  • At the start, both you and the dealer receive two cards.
  • One of the dealer’s cards is hidden until your turn ends.
  • You can choose:
    • Hit: Take another card to increase your total.
    • Stand: Keep your current total and end your turn.
  • You can take multiple hits until you stand or exceed 21.

Dealer Rules

  • After you stand, the dealer reveals the hidden card.
  • The dealer must draw cards until the total is at least 17.
  • The dealer stands on all 17s (including soft 17).

Winning Conditions

  • If your total exceeds 21, you lose (bust).
  • If the dealer exceeds 21, you win.
  • If neither busts:
    • Higher total wins.
    • Equal totals result in a tie (push).
